The Student Emergency Fund (SEF)

Description

The Student Emergency Fund (SEF) is designed to provide one-time limited emergency financial assistance (approximately $50 – $300) to Carleton University students experiencing short-term financial hardship related to critical needs.

When seeking student emergency funding, you will be connected to the Care and Support team to help assess your overall needs and they will assist you in navigating additional supports that may be useful in your situation.
