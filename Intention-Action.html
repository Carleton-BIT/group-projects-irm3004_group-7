From Intention to Action

Description

The From Intention to Action (FITA) program supports undergraduate and graduate students in the often stressful university experience. We help students to better manage stress and improve their academic performance, by navigating the personal stressors that can often get in the way of school.
FIT: Action students meet 1-on-1 with a Coordinator for 1 hour every week for either 5 or 10 consecutive weeks. Our Coordinators are staff members or Master’s-level interns trained in counseling and psychotherapy. Often students feel overwhelmed and aren’t sure where to go. The most common ways we help are supportive counseling, stress management, coping skills, time management, and learning strategies. Students must be considered full-time in order to be eligible for the FITA program.
