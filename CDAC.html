Carleton Disability Awareness Center (CDAC)

Description

CDAC is a peer support, advocacy, and community space for students who experience disability, chronic illness, neurodiversity, or inaccessibility. CDAC is designed to act as a forum for disability issues, providing events, and advocating for the issues important to the Carleton Community.
CDAC runs events such as distress with pets, sex and disability workshops, and fidget toy workshops.
