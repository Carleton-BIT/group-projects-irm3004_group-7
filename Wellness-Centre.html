Wellness Centre

Description

Welcome to the Wellness Centre, your destination for comprehensive support in nurturing your physical, mental, and emotional well-being.
We offer valuable peer-to-peer support, connecting you with fellow students who understand and empathize with your experiences. In addition, we provide a wealth of mental health resources to ensure you have access to the care and assistance you need. Your well-being is our top priority, which is why we also offer shelter resources and peer support training, ensuring you have the tools to navigate any challenges that may arise.
