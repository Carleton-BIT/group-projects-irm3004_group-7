Global Opportunities & International Student Services Office

Description

The Global Opportunities & International Student Services Office (GO-ISSO) is dedicated to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and supportive environment for international students while promoting cross-cultural engagement and global learning opportunities for all students at Carleton University.

The GO-ISSO offers services and programs that support all Carleton students in having enriching international experiences—by helping international and exchange students adjust to life in Canada and by encouraging global engagement through exchange, internships and study abroad opportunities.
