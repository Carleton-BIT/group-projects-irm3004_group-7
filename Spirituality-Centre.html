Spirituality Centre

Description

Carleton embraces the spirit of teamwork and coexistence, regardless of faith affiliation. Carleton recognizes the importance of spirituality to overall health and wellness and, as such, promotes an inclusive environment through supports, resources, and the Spirituality Centre.
