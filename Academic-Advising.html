Academic Advising

Description

We support undergraduate and special students to achieve academic success.
Advising sessions cover a variety of topics including : reviewing your academic progress, making program changes and connecting you with other student support services.

Contact

We’re open for drop-in advising Monday–Friday: 8:30 a.m.–11:30 a.m. 1:00 p.m.–3:30 p.m. Tip: Wait times are usually shorter in the morning.
You Can Also Email Us or Book a Virtual Appointment
For audit review or course selection assistance, please contact your Departmental Advisor directly.
For all other inquiries, you can contact us via email, or you can book a virtual appointment via MS Teams to speak with an academic advisor. We aim to respond to your inquiry within 3-5 business days.
