Wellness Desk

Description

Located on the main floor of the MacOdrum Library, the Wellness Desk is a unique drop-in space for students to learn about resources, connect with trained staff, and decompress during stressful times. The program’s goal is to help dismantle barriers to accessing supports where possible and contribute to the destigmatization of mental health care and discussions on wellness.
When you enter, you’ll be greeted by Wellness Desk staff, who are ready to support you with your mental health and wellness needs. You can chat about what areas you need support on, explore resources with the team, and walk away with services and/or resources to better support you.
Students may visit the Wellness Desk any time during its hours of operation – no appointments necessary!
