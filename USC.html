Unified Support Centre (USC)

Description

Welcome to the Unified Support Centre (USC), a department of Carleton University Students’ Association (CUSA). We are a volunteer-based space consisting of the Food Centre service cluster, providing on-campus food support and advocacy, and the Foot Patrol service cluster, offering evening-focused safety related support.
