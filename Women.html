Women’s Centre

Description

Welcome to the Women’s Learning, Advocacy, and Support Centre – a warm and welcoming space dedicated to providing support, education, and resources for individuals who identify as women, as well as anyone interested in gender-based issues.
We offer a range of essential resources, including free male/female condoms, pregnancy tests, and menstrual products, ensuring that everyone has access to the care they need. At the Women’s Centre, we take pride in offering a wealth of materials and information to foster learning and advocacy on gender-related matters.
