Breakfast Club

Description

Free Breakfast — No Questions Asked!
CUSA is committed to taking practical steps to address the food insecurity crisis at Carleton University. As such, we are proud to offer weekly breakfast free-of-charge for all students in The Wing and Ollie’s.

Breakfast Club

Join us weekly on a different day in the Wing for fresh fruit, granola bars, cereal, oatmeal, yogurt, cheese, and more!
The Wing: 8:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.

Pancake Breakfast

On the last Wednesday of the month, join us for some delicious pancakes before your morning class.
Ollie's Pub and Patio: 8:15 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.
