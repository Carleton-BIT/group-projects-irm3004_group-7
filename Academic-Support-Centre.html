Centre for Student Academic Support

Description

The Centre for Student Academic Support (CSAS) designs and delivers evidence-informed academic programs and services to support all Carleton University students in achieving academic success. CSAS provides tailored support with academic writing assignments and the development of study and learning skills.
Visit CSAS on the 4th floor of MacOdrum Library, open Monday to Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
